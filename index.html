Installation Guide

Getting a "damaged" error? Don't worry - this is normal for unsigned apps. Here's how to fix it.

"App is damaged" Error

macOS Gatekeeper protection

This is completely normal! macOS shows this misleading error for all unsigned apps, even legitimate software.

Your app is safe
No actual damage
Happens to all unsigned apps

Easy Fix

One command in Terminal

Step 1: Open Terminal (press Cmd+Space, type "Terminal")

Step 2: Copy and paste this command:

sudo xattr -rd com.apple.quarantine "/Applications/Proxy AI Assistant.app"

Step 3: Press Enter, enter your password, and you're done!

Complete Installation Steps

1

Download

Download the DMG file for your Mac type (Apple Silicon or Intel)

2

Install

Open DMG and drag Proxy AI Assistant to Applications folder

3

Fix & Launch

Run the Terminal command above, then launch the app normally

Why does this happen?

Apple requires developers to pay $99/year for code signing certificates. As an indie developer, we prioritize features over certificates. This command simply tells macOS to trust the app - it's completely safe and commonly used for indie software.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. Our AI assistant only captures screen and audio when YOU trigger it. No passive logging, no background recording. Your data stays on your device with our local-first architecture.
How does stealth mode work?
The assistant remains completely invisible during screen sharing, video calls, or when using multiple monitors. No overlays, no visible interface - just intelligent assistance when you need it.
What operating systems are supported?
We support Windows, Mac, and Linux with the same powerful features across all platforms. Cross-platform compatibility is a core part of our design.
How is this different from Rewind or Cluely?
Unlike passive tracking tools, we focus on real-time, intentional interaction. We don't log your life - we help you act smarter in the moment, only when you explicitly need assistance.
Can I use both voice and text input?
Yes! Our dual input system lets you speak your prompts or type them - whatever feels more natural for your situation. Perfect for meetings where you need to stay quiet or when voice is more convenient.
